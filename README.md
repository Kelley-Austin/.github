# Kelley Austin, a Perficient Company

**Salesforce implementation partner — Revenue Cloud, Agentforce, Data Cloud, and the integrations that hold it all together.**

We design and build Salesforce solutions for mid-market and enterprise teams that have outgrown out-of-the-box. Our engineers and architects work alongside client teams from discovery through deployment and beyond — no offshore handoffs, no template decks, no surprises at go-live.

## What we work on

**Revenue Cloud & CPQ** — Quote-to-cash, subscription billing, and pricing logic for businesses with complex product catalogs and sales motions. Migrations from legacy CPQ to Revenue Cloud, CPQ+ implementations, and the constraint modeling and bundle work that actually holds up in production.

**Agentforce & AI** — Production-grade Agentforce Service deployments, Einstein and prompt-builder customization, and the data and trust layer work that determines whether an AI rollout sticks. We focus on use cases with measurable cycle-time or deflection impact rather than pilots that stall.

**Data Cloud & Analytics** — Data Cloud activations, identity resolution, calculated insights, and downstream activation into Marketing Cloud, Agentforce, and Tableau / Tableau Next. We pair this with CRM Analytics work for teams that need both operational and decision-support reporting.

**Integrations** — Mulesoft, Celigo, and native API integrations connecting Salesforce to ERP, billing, and data platforms. We treat integration as a first-class part of the architecture, not an afterthought at UAT.

## How we work

- Senior-led teams. The architects and leads on the proposal are the ones doing the work.
- Documented decisions. Every engagement leaves the client with the design artifacts to maintain and extend what we built.
- Salesforce-native delivery. SFDX, scratch orgs, source-driven deployments, and tested release pipelines.
